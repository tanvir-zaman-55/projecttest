Fried Rice with Beef and Teriyaki Chicken

Ingredients

1 cup cooked white rice (preferably chilled) 1/2 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced 1/2 lb chicken breast, thinly sliced 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce 2 eggs, beaten 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn) 3 green onions, finely chopped 3 tablespoons soy sauce 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 1 teaspoon sesame oil Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

Step 1: Prepare Ingredients Ensure that the rice is cooked and cooled. You can use leftover rice for better results. Thinly slice the beef and chicken. Chop the green onions and prepare the mixed vegetables. Step 2: Cook Beef Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside. Step 3: Cook Chicken In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of oil. Cook the sliced chicken until it's no longer pink. Remove from the skillet and set aside. Step 4: Scramble Eggs Push the remaining contents of the skillet to one side and pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked. Step 5: Combine Ingredients Add the chilled rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps. Pour the teriyaki sauce, soy sauce, and sesame oil over the rice. Stir to combine. Add the cooked beef, chicken, scrambled eggs, mixed vegetables, and chopped green onions to the skillet. Stir well to combine all ingredients. Step 6: Adjust Seasoning Taste the fried rice and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper, as needed. Step 7: Serve Once everything is heated through and well combined, remove the skillet from heat. Serve the fried rice hot, garnished with additional chopped green onions if desired. Enjoy your delicious Fried Rice with Beef and Teriyaki Chicken!
